What Is Kroll Developments?

Kroll Developments is a business that focuses on coding custom, or requested systems and websites in JavaScript, HTML, and Lua.


We build scripts and tools for game servers, bots, and web interfaces, helping communities and projects get exactly what they need with clean, maintainable code.


Whether you need a FiveM system, a Discord bot, or a custom web panel, Kroll Developments designs and develops solutions tailored to your idea.
